Once you have purchased your Shopify theme from Shopledge you should customize according to your needs. Customizing should take from a couple of minutes to maximum one hour.

Add Your Logo

After installing the theme purchased your shop header section might look like this: [caption id="attachment_293" align="alignnone" width="500" caption="Your Theme Header"][/caption] Either you are keeping your site without logo, as is, or you upload a small logo and replace your shop name. We recommend to use a small, standard-sized logo of 120x60 pixels. You can check out the theme cheatsheet for more information. Navigate to your Admin Area → Theme → Theme Settings and make your modifications. [caption id="attachment_298" align="alignnone" width="500" caption="Setting up your logo"][/caption]

Adjust Header Background and Colors

Perhaps the original theme design will be altered by your logo, your brand identity. No problem you can adjust every color of your theme. It is important to keep the color harmony -- if you have no designer to consult you, use Adobe's color generator to choose analogous, complementary, compound or similar colors. [caption id="attachment_316" align="alignnone" width="500" caption="Setting the appearance of your header"][/caption]

Manage Your Main Menu

In the header section, at left hand side, you'll find your default main Main Menu created by Shopify. These default items -- Home, Catalog, Blog, About Us -- are usually enough, but, we suggest you should consider these best practices and update your menu bar. We suggest add two or three new menu items: Contact, Support, How to Buy? (or join the first two items into Contact and Support) and collect them into a new link list called Shopping Menu. (this name is mandatory otherwise the theme won't recognize it). All this is a three step operation as follows:
  1. Create the pages in Admin Area → Blogs & Pages → Create a new page.
  2. Create a new link list in Admin Area → Navigation → Add link list.
  3. Add the pages to the link list created , in Admin Area → Navigation → Shopping Menu and Add link.
